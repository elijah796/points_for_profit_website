Points For Profit

Shop Local. Serve Local. Sustain Local.

Shop Local

Shopping local keeps your community strong, your neighbors employed, and your dollars working where they matter most. When you support local, you’re not just buying a product—you’re investing in the heart and future of your community.

Serve Local

because strong communities are built by the hands and hearts within them. When you serve your neighbors, you create an impact that ripples far beyond today.

Sustain Local

when we shop locally and serve locally, we create a cycle that keeps our communities thriving. Together, we preserve what makes our towns special—today, tomorrow, and for generations to come.

Who We Are

The heart of our mission is a simple but powerful belief: we all need each other—every person matters (yes, our pets do too). When we choose Shop Local, we invest in our neighbors' dreams. When we Serve Local, we lift one another up through kindness, generosity, and shared purpose. When we do both consistently, we Sustain Local—creating a thriving, connected community that supports itself, grows together, and stands strong through every season.

This movement was built on faith, love, and action. While we are rooted in Christian values, we don’t require statements of faith—we simply strive to be a light in how we live, work, and give. Through every connection we make and every opportunity we create, our goal is to reflect hope, unity, and genuine care for others. Because when people come together with open hearts and shared purpose, something truly meaningful happens—we build a community where everyone belongs.
